Carleone-Aquarin Poké Mart

For all your Poké needs

Aspear Berry:
Held Item: A Berry to be consumed by Pokémon. If a Pokémon holds one, it can recover from being frozen on its own in battle.
Ingredient: A Berry that gives off a sour fragrance when you bite it. Using a lot of them adds a light aftertaste to a dish.

Armor Fossil:
A fossil from a prehistoric Pokémon that lived on the land. It appears to be part of a head.

Nanab Berry:
A berry that will stop a Pokémon's movement during encounter, making it easier to capture on the Trainer's next attempt.

Bluk Berry:
A Berry to be used in cooking. This Berry is very rare and hard to obtain in the Unova region
Used to make Pokéblocks that will enhance your Beauty. Its blue flesh is sour when eaten.
